About BedTime Story (BS)

- Welcome to BS (BedTime Story), where creativity meets storytelling in a whole new way! Our innovative application brings together the power of images and words to craft captivating narratives that span a spectrum of emotions.

- With BS, you can effortlessly collect multiple images and generate captivating captions for each one. But we don't stop there! Our unique feature allows you to seamlessly blend these generated captions into a cohesive story, infused with various moods such as humor, melancholy, excitement, and more.

- Whether you're looking to ignite laughter, tug at heartstrings, or simply express yourself in a new light, BS empowers you to create immersive stories that resonate with your audience. Unleash your imagination, unleash the storyteller within, with BS.

Tech Stacks Used:
  • Frontend: TailwindCSS (CSS FrameWork)
  • Backend: FastAPI (Python FrameWork)
  • Machine Learning: PyTorch (Python FrameWork)
